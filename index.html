  • Netela

Transportation & Parking

Friday Transportation

Arriving before 12:00 PM:

Direct transport to assigned dorms for check-in, then shuttle to church at 1PM

Arriving after 12:00 PM:

Direct transport to church for program start. Dorm check-in after evening events

Driving & Parking

Location: University Commons Parking Deck
Address: 204 John Wesley Dobbs, Atlanta, GA 30303

Instructions:

  1. Press button on grey pad at entrance
  2. Tell security your name and mention "Campus Ministry General Assembly"
  3. Drive to Level P1 (4th floor)
  4. Knock on double doors to enter lobby
  5. Have your license plate number ready for registration
